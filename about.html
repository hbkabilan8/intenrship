About BubbleHouse

Our mission is to make real estate transactions simple, transparent, and enjoyable

Our Story

Founded in 2015, BubbleHouse began with a simple idea: real estate doesn't have to be complicated. Our founders, experienced real estate professionals, saw an opportunity to create a more client-focused approach to buying and selling homes.

What started as a small team in Los Angeles has grown into a national real estate platform serving thousands of clients each year. Despite our growth, we've maintained our commitment to personalized service and innovative solutions.

We believe everyone deserves a stress-free real estate experience, whether they're buying their first home or selling a luxury property. Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to make that belief a reality for every client.
